What is Ocuflow?

Ocuflow is the first in-vitro platform to simultaneously simulate tear flow, tear volume, and blinking through air exposure and mechanical rubbing.

See Ocuflow in Action

Features

Simulate

Simulate tear volume, tear flow, and blinking.

Programmable

Programmable tear-flow and blinking rates.

Modular

Modular and upgradable components to meet your research needs.

Versatile

Versatile eyepieces adaptable for various in-vitro analyses.

Applications

A universal in-vitro platform to provide new insights in multiple areas of contact lens research.

Measuring release of drugs or wetting agents from contact lenses.
Testing novel contact lenses and ophthalmic materials.
Evaluating tear deposition on contact lenses.
Measuring cytotoxicity of contact lenses.
Evaluating microbial contamination of contact lenses.
Inexpensive optometric eye model to teach and practice foreign body removal.
